1- Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines. Use your own words.

The video says that being smart alone is not enough to succeed. What matters more is grit, which means keeping going and not giving up on your goals. Angela Duckworth shows through examples and research that people who don't quit easily do better in life.

2- Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines in your own words.

The video says that doing a little work every day is better than waiting for the right mood or depending on luck. Being regular and disciplined matters more than anything else.

3- What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal Locus of Control means you believe that your results depend on your own actions and decisions, not on luck or other people.
The main point of the video is that when people stop blaming others and start working on themselves, they improve and stay motivated.

4- What are the key points mentioned by the speaker to build a growth mindset?

The speaker mentioned these points:
a- Believe that you can improve with effort.
b- Don't stick to fixed thinking, question it.
c- Find your own way to learn things.
d- Don't avoid hard situations, learn from them.

5- What are your ideas to take action and build a Growth Mindset?

My ideas are:
a- It's okay to make mistakes, that's how we learn.
b- Practice daily, even when you don't feel like it.
c- Try new things instead of always doing what is easy.
d- Listen to feedback and use it to get better.
e- Stop saying "I can't do this" and start saying "I will learn this."
f- Stay calm when things are difficult.
g- Set small goals and work on them one by one.
h- See what successful people do and try to follow their habits.
i- Focus on getting better slowly instead of wanting fast results.
j- Always be ready to learn something new.
